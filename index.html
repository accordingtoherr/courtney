About Me

Courtney Clute is a writer and professional. She completed her MFA in fiction at the University of South Florida. She also holds a Professional and Technical Communication Graduate Certificate. At USF, she won the Hirshberg Award for Graduate Achievement. Courtney's flash fiction has been nominated for Best of the Net 2021 and Best Small Fictions 2021 and 2022. She is a reader for Fractured Literary and recently completed a reading residency for SmokeLong Quarterly.

Her work has appeared in Passages North, Fractured Literary, Emerge Literary Journal, Flash Fiction Magazine, The Lumiere Review, Gulf Stream Literary Magazine, Ekphrastic Review, Flash Frog, Parentheses Journal, and Z Publishing’s Florida’s Emerging Writers: An Anthology. You can find her on Twitter at @courtney_clute.
