Courtney Clute

About Me

Courtney Clute is a writer and professional. She completed her MFA in fiction at the University of South Florida in Tampa, Florida. She also holds a Professional and Technical Communication Graduate Certificate. At USF, she won the Hirshberg Award for Graduate Achievement.

Her work has appeared in Passages North, Z Publishing’s Florida’s Emerging Writers: An Anthology, and elsewhere. You can find her on Twitter at @courtney_clute.
